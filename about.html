I'm an artist, designer, and educator in Chicago.

I like making things, and I like helping people make things. Science and language are two common themes in my art, sometimes together. I've been making art since before I can remember, and I have over three years of experience as an interaction and data visualization designer in the IOT/analytics space. I've taught all ages, including undergraduates at the School of the Art Institute of Chicago, where I received an MFA in Art and Technology Studies.
